Privacy and Policy

We collect personal information from you, including information about your:

  • location
  • interactions with us
  • billing or purchase information

We collect your personal information in order to:

  • register customers
  • confirm online payment

Besides our staff, we share this information with:

  • PayPal third-party in order to confirm online payment.

Providing information:

Providing some information is optional. If you choose not to enter a credit card information, we'll be unable to provide online ordering services.

Information safety:

We keep your information safe by storing it in encrypted files and only allowing admin to access it.

We keep your information for two years at which point we securely destroy it by securely erase all digital information.
